Aēsop

is a global skin care brand owned by Brazilian company Natura & Co.
In addition to skin care, Aesop also produces hair care, soaps and fragrance.
Each Aesop store has a unique interior design developed in collaboration with various architects, interior designers and artists. As of 2019, the brand had over 320 points of sale across 25 countries. Aesop produces 83 product formulations and three shaving accessories.
